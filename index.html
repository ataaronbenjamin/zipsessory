What is Zipsessory?

Zipsessory is a jewellery pendant that clips onto any zipper pull, transforming an ordinary zipper into a sparkling style statement.

It also solves a real problem: the loop lets you reach a centre-back dress zipper without acrobatic contortions. Fashion and function.
